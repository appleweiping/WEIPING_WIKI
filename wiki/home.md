## Operating Rules

- Fast answer first, durable ingest second.
- Public `wiki/` is the website source; private/raw layers stay out of the public build.
- Use live rescans before current-state claims about external local roots.
- For abstract or general research/project prompts, look outward to strong GitHub projects and top-conference paper projects for inspiration, then synthesize without copying.
- Do not delete information without explicit user approval; preserve useful old information through annotation, archiving, or merging.

## How This Site Works

The public site is built with Quartz from the maintained Markdown wiki. It preserves wikilinks, search, backlinks, tags, and graph navigation while keeping raw and private materials out of the published artifact.
