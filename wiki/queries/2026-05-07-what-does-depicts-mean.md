# What Does Depicts Mean

## Question

What does `depicts` mean?

## Short Answer

`Depicts` means:

- depicts = shows
- depicts = describes
- depicts = portrays
- depicts = represents

In Chinese:

- `depicts` = 描绘
- `depicts` = 描述
- `depicts` = 展示
- `depicts` = 表现出

## Grammar

`Depicts` is the third-person singular present form of the verb `depict`.

Examples:

```text
This image depicts a robot exploring Venus.
```

Meaning:

```text
这张图描绘了一个机器人在探索金星。
```

```text
The diagram depicts the relationship between the roles.
```

Meaning:

```text
这个图表展示了这些角色之间的关系。
```

## Common Pattern

Use `depicts` when a picture, diagram, story, film, or text shows or describes something.

Common phrases:

- `This image depicts ...`
- `The diagram depicts ...`
- `The story depicts ...`
- `The painting depicts ...`
