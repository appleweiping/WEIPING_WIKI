## Summary

ulogme How productive were you today? How much code have you written? Where did your time go? Keep track of your computer activity throughout the day: visualize your active window titles and the number of keystrokes in beautiful HTML timelines. Current features: - Records your active window title throughout the day - Records the frequency of key presses throughout the day - Record custom note annotations for particular times of day, or for day in general - Everything runs completely locally : none of your data is uploaded anywhere - Beautiful, customizable UI in HTML/CSS/JS (d3js).
